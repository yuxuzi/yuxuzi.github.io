Getting to NYC

Getting to NYC is convenient and affordable from 65 Condict St! We’re connected to multiple transit options, allowing you to pick what works best for your budget and schedule. Here’s a quick guide to help you decide:

1. PATH Train (Most Popular)

The PATH train from Journal Square is the fastest and most popular way to reach NYC. It’s just a 5-10 minute trip from our location to Journal Square, where you can hop on the PATH and be in Manhattan in under 20 minutes. With frequent service and affordable rates, PATH is ideal for most visitors.

  • - Budget-Friendly Choice (Bus 1/14/80)

    For a low-cost trip, take Bus #1, #14, or #80 to Journal Square for only $1.80. The bus stop is just a 5-7 minute walk away. These buses run frequently and reach Journal Square in just 10 minutes, making it easy to connect to the PATH train and continue to NYC.

  • - Rideshare Shuttle (Via App)

    For a budget-friendly, app-based option, download the Via app and catch a shared ride to Journal Square for around $2. While convenient, arrival times may vary during rush hours.

  • - Drive Yourself

    Prefer driving? It’s a quick 5-10 minute drive to Journal Square, where parking options are available. From there, you can catch the PATH train or explore additional parking in Manhattan if you choose to drive all the way.

  • - Ride-Hail Services (Uber/Lyft)

    Hail an Uber or Lyft directly to Journal Square or NYC, with fares generally between $6 and $20 depending on traffic and distance. This option is flexible and convenient if you’re looking for a private ride.

2. Bus #119 to NYC Port Authority

The #119 bus takes you directly to NYC’s Port Authority Bus Terminal for $4.00. The bus stop is a quick 10-minute walk from our location, and the trip takes about an hour, making it a comfortable, direct option to reach midtown Manhattan.

Exploring Jersey City

Want to explore Jersey City? Here’s a great way to get around locally:

City Bike

Rent a City Bike from the station at Kensington Ave & West Side Ave, just a short walk away. Rates start at $4.79 for 30 minutes, or $19 for a full day. It’s a fun, affordable way to explore Jersey City at your own pace!
